# 📊 Telco Customer Churn Analysis

A complete Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) project on the Telco Customer Churn dataset using Python. This project analyzes customer behavior and identifies the major factors contributing to customer churn through statistical analysis and visualizations.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges faced by subscription-based businesses. Understanding why customers leave helps organizations develop better retention strategies.

This project performs an in-depth analysis of customer demographics, services, payment methods, contracts, and tenure to discover patterns associated with customer churn.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze customer churn behavior.
- Identify the major factors influencing churn.
- Explore relationships between customer demographics and churn.
- Generate business insights through visualizations.
- Provide recommendations to improve customer retention.

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 📌 Overall Churn
- Around **26.5%** of customers have churned.

### 📌 Contract Type
- Customers with **Month-to-Month contracts** show the highest churn.
- One-year and two-year contracts have significantly lower churn rates.

### 📌 Payment Method
- Customers using **Electronic Check** are more likely to churn than those using Credit Card or Bank Transfer.

### 📌 Customer Tenure
- Customers with shorter tenure are much more likely to leave.
- Long-term customers generally remain loyal.

### 📌 Senior Citizens
- Senior citizens exhibit a relatively higher churn rate compared to non-senior customers.

### 📌 Additional Services
Customers with the following services tend to have lower churn:

- Online Security
- Tech Support
- Device Protection
- Online Backup
